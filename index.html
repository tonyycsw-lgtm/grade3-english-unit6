學習英文日記寫作與動物描述：

海洋公園遊記

29th December, 2018 (Sat)

I went to Ocean Park with my friends, Joe, Sue, Mike and Anna. The weather was windy and cool. We went there by bus. We were thrilled because we could see many land and sea animals there.
2018年12月29日（星期六）

我和我的朋友Joe、Sue、Mike和Anna去了海洋公園。天氣多風且涼爽。我們乘巴士去那裡。我們非常興奮，因為我們可以在那裡看到許多陸地和海洋動物。
I love giant pandas best. I always want to know more about giant pandas. Anna gave me an information leaflet about giant pandas.
我最喜歡大熊貓。我一直想了解更多關於大熊貓的知識。Anna給了我一份關於大熊貓的資料單張。
Giant pandas have black and white fur. The fur around their eyes and on their ears, legs and shoulders is black. They have a round face, a big head, a large body and short legs. A giant panda can grow up to 1.5 meters long and weigh up to 110 kilograms.
大熊貓有黑白相間的皮毛。他們眼睛周圍、耳朵、腿和肩膀上的皮毛是黑色的。他們有一個圓臉、大頭、大身體和短腿。一隻大熊貓可以長到1.5米長，重達110公斤。
Most giant pandas live in the forests in Sichuan. They like to eat bamboo. They have large paws so they can climb up bamboo trees and hold on to the tree trunks.
大多數大熊貓生活在四川的森林裡。他們喜歡吃竹子。他們有大爪子，所以可以爬上竹子並抓住樹幹。
Giant pandas are big but their babies are tiny. At birth, baby giant pandas weigh about 100 grams. Baby giant pandas are called cubs. Cubs are pink and have no fur.
大熊貓很大，但他們的寶寶卻很小。出生時，大熊貓寶寶重約100克。大熊貓寶寶被稱為幼崽。幼崽是粉紅色的，沒有皮毛。
There are not many giant pandas now, so people keep giant pandas in zoos and reserves to take good care of them. In Hong Kong, there are four giant pandas in Ocean Park.
現在大熊貓數量不多，所以人們把大熊貓養在動物園和保護區裡，好好照顧他們。在香港，海洋公園有四隻大熊貓。
